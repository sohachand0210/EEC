Energy Efficiency Community (EEC)

About:

The Energy Efficiency Community (EEC), established in 2024, is a community where people aim at improving the lifestyle of the overall campus of the college, taking the natural ecosystem into consideration.

This is a community, which adds a reasonable value to the person's lifestyle, as well as the contribution of people would make great impacts on the campus.

The first branch of EEC was started at NMIMS MPSTME, which is located at Mumbai, Maharashtra.

Aim:

Our aim is to promote energy efficiency and build projects focused on energy conservation.

The aim of the community is to bring together all the people of similar interests, and make the most use of the environment, and at the same timwe, to not harm it for our benefits.
