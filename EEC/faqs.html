Top Questions

Q: What is the main goal of the Energy Efficiency Committee?

A: The main goal of the Energy Efficiency Committee is to improve energy efficiency of the built environment on campus, with a focus on cooling requirements.

Q: What is the first project of the Energy Efficiency Committee?

A: The first project of the Energy Efficiency Committee is to build Solar Panels on the Terrace of the building, with a transparent layer above them to drain water and produce electricity.

Q: How will the committee use the collected rainwater?

A: The committee plans to use the collected rainwater for drinking purposes after proper processing, or for harvesting and washing purposes.

Q: What is the plan for capturing humidity during winter season?

A: The committee plans to build a hardware or navigation system to capture humidity and produce electricity using the total humidity pressure.

Q: How will the committee use drones during summer season?

A: The committee plans to use drones to sprinkle water from the skies, while checking for factors like heat, temperature, and humidity to ensure the correct amount of water is sprinkled.
