Our Projects

1.) Solar Panel System

Dynamic solar panels with water drainage and rainwater harvesting system. The solar panels will be installed on the terrace of the building and will have a technology that allows them to change their angle and area of range according to the time and placement of the sun. A transparent layer above the panels will help to drain water during rainy season, which can be used for drinking purposes or harvesting and washing purposes after proper processing.

2.) Humidity Electricity Generation

Using high humidity levels in winter to generate electricity through pressure systems. A hardware or navigation system will be built to capture humidity and convert it into electricity using the total humidity pressure.

3.) Summer Cooling via Drone Water Sprinkling

Drones equipped with sensors to sprinkle water for campus cooling. The drones will be programmed to check for various factors like heat, temperature, humidity, etc. and sprinkle the correct amount of water to cool the campus during summer.
